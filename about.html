About EchoBoard

Our Mission

EchoBoard is built to amplify every employee’s voice. In a world where workplace feedback often goes unheard, we aim to provide a safe, transparent, and effective platform to share concerns, praise, and suggestions.

Why EchoBoard?

Whether you're an employee with an idea or an admin working to improve culture, EchoBoard ensures every message counts. We are committed to improving communication, trust, and action within organizations of all sizes.

Meet the Team

We're a small group of developers, designers, and workplace advocates who believe in the power of feedback. EchoBoard is our way of bringing real change to how teams communicate.
