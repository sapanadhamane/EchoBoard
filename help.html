Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I submit feedback?

A: Go to the Feedback page, fill in the details, and click Submit.

Q2: Can I give anonymous feedback?

A: Yes, just leave the name field empty in the feedback form.

Q3: Where can I see my past feedback?

A: Log in and go to the Employee Dashboard. Your submitted feedbacks are listed there.

Getting Started

  • 🔐 Log in using your Employee ID and Password
  • 📝 Use the feedback form to report issues or give suggestions
  • 👀 Track responses in your Dashboard
